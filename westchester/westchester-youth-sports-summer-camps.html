Youth Sports Summer Camps in Westchester County

Westchester County offers a rich summer sports camp landscape — from half-day single-sport skills camps to full-day programs combining multiple sports with traditional camp activities. With some of the Northeast's finest outdoor facilities and proximity to major universities and sports academies, Westchester families have excellent options at every budget level.

Book early: Popular Westchester summer sports camps fill by February–March. Don't wait until May — top programs sell out, especially college-hosted and lacrosse-specific camps.

Types of Summer Sports Camps in Westchester

Single-sport skills camps (Half-day)

Focused instruction for one sport, typically 9am–12pm or 9am–1pm. Lacrosse, soccer, baseball, and tennis are most common in Westchester. Cost: $150–$350/week.

Full-day sports day camps

9am–3pm or 9am–4pm format combining sports instruction with general camp activities. Best for working parents. Cost: $350–$650/week.

University-hosted camps

Manhattanville University, SUNY Purchase, Westchester Community College, and nearby Fordham and Columbia all run or host summer sports camps. High-quality instruction at moderate prices. $250–$500/week.

Elite club camps

Travel clubs run week-long summer camps open to club members and the public. High competition level. Good evaluation opportunity for players considering joining the club.

Summer Camp Registration Tips for Westchester Families

  • Register by March 1 for top programs — lacrosse and soccer camps in particular fill fast
  • Ask about sibling discounts — most camps offer 10% off for a second child
  • Check coach-to-player ratios — ideal is 1:6 to 1:10 for skills camps
  • Look for shade — Westchester summers are hot and humid; outdoor camps need adequate shade and hydration breaks
  • Multi-week discounts are common — many camps offer 10–15% off for 2+ consecutive weeks
  • Financial assistance — contact Westchester County Parks and your town parks department for subsidized summer sports programs
Frequently Asked Questions — Youth Sports Summer Camps
When do Westchester summer sports camps open registration?▾
Most top Westchester summer sports camps open registration in January–February. Many popular programs sell out by March–April. Set a reminder for January 1 and register as soon as programs open — especially for lacrosse, soccer, and baseball camps.
How much do sports summer camps cost in Westchester County?▾
Half-day skills camps in Westchester run $150–$350/week. Full-day sports day camps run $400–$700/week. University-hosted programs are typically $250–$500/week. Multi-week and sibling discounts are common.
Are there multi-sport summer camps in Westchester County?▾
Yes — several Westchester parks departments and the YMCA offer multi-sport summer programs ideal for younger children (ages 4–10) who haven't chosen a sport yet. These programs typically cover soccer, baseball, basketball, and flag football in a fun, low-pressure setting at $150–$300/week.
What is the best sports summer camp in Westchester County?▾
The best camp depends on the sport and your child's age. University-hosted camps offer strong instruction at fair prices. Single-sport skills camps run by travel clubs offer the highest intensity. For multi-sport younger kids, town parks programs are excellent value. Use YouthSportsConnect to find current summer camp listings near you.
Do Westchester sports summer camps offer financial assistance?▾
Some do — Westchester County Parks, town parks departments, and PAL-affiliated programs offer subsidized summer programs for qualifying families. Contact your municipality's parks department directly, as assistance programs vary by town.
