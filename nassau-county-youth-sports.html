Youth Sports in Nassau County, NY

Nassau County is one of the most active youth sports communities in New York. With dozens of towns from Massapequa to Great Neck, and a dense network of leagues, travel clubs, and training facilities, Nassau County families have more options — and more decisions to make — than almost anywhere else on Long Island.

Nassau County Youth Soccer

Nassau County is home to some of Long Island's most competitive LIJSL clubs. Massapequa SC and East Meadow SC consistently rank among the best clubs in the country. The county has strong representation at every level from recreational PAL programs through ECNL and MLS NEXT. LIJSL travel tryouts for Nassau County clubs typically run May–June.

Nassau County Lacrosse

Nassau County PAL lacrosse is one of the largest youth lacrosse programs in the country, operating 31 units across 40+ communities. On the travel side, Nassau County produces more D1 lacrosse commits per capita than almost any region in the US. Programs like Express Lacrosse, Team 91, and Garden City Lacrosse have built national reputations.

Nassau County Baseball

Little League baseball is deeply rooted in Nassau County communities — from Bellmore to Massapequa Coast to Rockville Centre. Travel baseball organizations like LI Huskies, HEAT Baseball, and Nassau Baseball Club field competitive teams from 8U through 18U competing in USABL, Perfect Game, and showcase events.
