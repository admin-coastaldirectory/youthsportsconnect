Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026 · Coastal Directory, LLC · State of Wyoming

This Privacy Policy describes how YouthSportsConnect ("we," "us," or "our"), owned and operated by Coastal Directory, LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company, collects, uses, and shares information when you visit youthsportsconnect.co (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide: When you submit a listing, you provide your name, email address, organization name, phone number, and website URL. This information is stored in our database and used to populate your listing.
  • Usage data: We may collect standard web analytics data including pages visited, time on site, browser type, and referring URLs through third-party analytics tools.
  • Email address: If you subscribe to our newsletter or submit a listing, we collect your email address.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and display your listing on the Site
  • To contact you about your listing submission or updates
  • To send you newsletters or sports updates if you opt in
  • To improve the Site and understand how visitors use it

3. Information Shared Publicly

If you submit a listing, the following information may be displayed publicly on the Site: organization name, email address, phone number, website URL, address, and social media links. Do not submit contact information you do not want displayed publicly.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data independently:

  • Supabase — database hosting for listing data
  • Netlify — website hosting
  • Google Analytics — website traffic analytics (if enabled)
  • Amazon Associates — affiliate links to Amazon products

Each third party has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

5. Affiliate Links

This Site participates in affiliate programs including the Amazon Associates program. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. See our Affiliate Disclosure for full details.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies for analytics and functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the Site may not function properly as a result.

7. Data Retention

Listing data is retained indefinitely unless you request removal. To request that your listing or personal information be removed, contact us at hello@youthsportsconnect.co.

8. Children's Privacy

This Site is a directory intended for parents, coaches, and sports organizations. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to our Site, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at hello@youthsportsconnect.co. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
